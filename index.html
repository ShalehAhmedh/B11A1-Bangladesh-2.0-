Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Technological innovation is the process of creating new, improved technologies problems or anything.

Sustainability

A nation's sustainability is the ability to meet the needs of the present, past or future without compromising.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the basic facilities and system serving a country, location, area, region, or community.

Empowerment

Empowering people means giving them the resources and tools they need to be very much successful.

The Vision of Future

A vision of the future is a personal aspiration or a description of a desired future for society. It can be a small personal goal or a large-scale societal change.

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation is the process of using digital technologies to change a business's processes, culture, and customer experience.

Green Energy

Green energy is energy that comes from renewable sources and has the greatest environmental benefit. It's also known as renewable energy.

Modern Infastructure

Modern infrastructure is a collection of technologies and services that help businesses design, manage, and deploy their IT systems.
